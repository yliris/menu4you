Our History:

"Menu4You" is a trend that blooms whenever, but not everyone knows the context or history behind it. Menu4You aims to delight its customers with its selection of drinks and delicious desserts. Though the exact date on which the word was used as such cannot be determined, most restaurants do them for the sake of their customers.

Summary of Menu4You:

2019

The cafe's opening

During late 2019, the shop opened in Amadeo, Cavite.

2020

Cafe closure as a result of the pandemic

In early 2020, when the pandemic happened, the shop was forced to close for almost a year.

February 2022

Café's reopening

On February 10, 2022, the shop reopened and added new items to the menu, namely Creamy Matcha.

December 2022

Deliveries are offered

On December 28, 2022, Menu 4 You became accessible to Grab and Food Panda users.

April 2023

Founded a branch in Silang

On April 7, 2023, the branch in Silang, Cavite was established.

September 2023

Carmona branch opened

On September 2, 2023, the branch in Carmona, Cavite was established.

June 2024

The public can access the café website

On June 11, 2024, the website of Menu4You was opened to the public.
